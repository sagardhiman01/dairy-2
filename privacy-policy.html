Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

At Vaishnavi Mava Bhandar, we value the privacy of our customers and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order.
  • Payment information processed securely through our payment partners (Razorpay).
  • Communication history when you contact our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your orders for fresh dairy products.
  • To send order updates and delivery notifications.
  • To improve our products and services based on customer feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent transactions.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. Your payment information is encrypted and processed through secure gateways. We do not store your credit card or UPI details on our servers.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share necessary data with service providers like delivery partners and payment processors (Razorpay) to complete your transactions.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your cart items, and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to do so, please contact us at the details provided below.
