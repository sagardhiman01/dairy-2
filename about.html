Our Story

A Family Legacy of Pure Dairy

Vaishnavi Mava Bhandar was born from a simple belief — every family deserves access to pure, fresh, and unadulterated dairy products. What started as a small family farm has grown into a trusted name serving thousands of families across Delhi NCR and Haryana.

We don't use preservatives, chemicals, or shortcuts. Our cows are grass-fed, healthy, and treated with love. Every litre of milk, every kilogram of paneer, and every spoonful of ghee carries the legacy of three generations of dairy farming.
