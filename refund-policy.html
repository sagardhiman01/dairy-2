Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Since Vaishnavi Mava Bhandar deals in fresh and perishable food items, our return and refund policy is governed by the nature of the products.

1. Cancellation Policy

  • You can cancel your order at any time before it has been dispatched from our facility.
  • Once the order is out for delivery, cancellations are not permitted.
  • To cancel, please contact us immediately at +91 88818 56426.

2. Return Policy

  • Being perishable goods, we do not accept returns once the product has been delivered and accepted by the customer.
  • However, if you receive a product that is damaged or spoiled at the time of delivery, please return it to the delivery executive immediately.

3. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are only applicable in cases where:
    • The order was cancelled before dispatch.
    • The product was found to be damaged/spoiled at the time of delivery and was rejected.
    • The wrong item was delivered.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within **5-7 business days** to the original payment method (Bank Account/UPI/Wallet) via Razorpay.

4. Quality Issues

If you notice any quality issue after delivery (e.g., milk getting spoiled within its shelf life despite proper storage), please contact us with details and photos. We will investigate and provide a suitable resolution (replacement or refund) after verification.
