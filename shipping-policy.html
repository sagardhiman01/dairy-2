Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

At Vaishnavi Mava Bhandar, we ensure that your fresh dairy products reach you in the shortest possible time. Please read our delivery policy below:

1. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver across:

  • Delhi NCR (Delhi, Noida, Ghaziabad, Meerut, Baghpat)
  • Select parts of Haryana

2. Delivery Timeline

Since we deal in perishable goods, we have a fixed delivery schedule to maintain freshness:

  • Morning Delivery: 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM
  • Evening Delivery: 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM

Orders placed before 8 PM are usually delivered in the next morning slot. Minimum delivery time is 12-24 hours depending on the order time.

3. Shipping Charges

We provide **FREE DELIVERY** on all orders with a minimum value of ₹1000. Orders below this value are currently not accepted for home delivery.

4. Delivery Process

Our delivery executive will contact you once they reach your location. Please ensure someone is available to receive the delivery to maintain the product's cold chain.

5. Delays

While we strive to meet our timelines, delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances like heavy rain, traffic, or logistics issues. We will inform you in case of any significant delay.

6. Packaging

All products are packed in food-grade materials to ensure safety and hygiene during transit.

7. Contact Support

For any delivery-related queries, please call us at +91 88818 56426.
